The Problem

India's vets are running modern practices on paper registers and WhatsApp screenshots.

  • No patient records Vaccination history, treatment plans, and visit notes live in physical files — or nowhere at all.
  • No appointment system Scheduling happens over phone calls. Cancellations cascade into hours of wasted chair time.
  • No billing visibility Revenue leaks through untracked invoices and manual payment reconciliation.
  • No pet-parent connection The relationship ends at the clinic door. No reminders, no follow-ups, no community.
  • No supply intelligence Clinics discover stockouts when vaccines run out mid-appointment.
The Product
Mia is the practice management platform built ground-up for Indian vets.

WhatsApp-native. Mobile-first. Priced for the market. Nine modules, zero clutter.
